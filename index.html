Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the benefit of choosing concrete over other materials?

+
Concrete offers exceptional durability, longevity, and requires minimal maintenance compared to asphalt or pavers. It can withstand heavy loads and harsh Ohio weather conditions, making it a cost-effective choice in the long run. Additionally, concrete allows for a wide range of decorative options to enhance aesthetic appeal.

How long does a concrete project typically take?

+
Project timelines vary depending on size, complexity, and weather conditions. A typical driveway or patio installation might take anywhere from a few days to a week for preparation, pouring, and initial curing. We'll provide a detailed timeline during your initial consultation.

How long until I can use my new concrete surface?

+
While concrete can be walked on within 24â48 hours, it typically takes 7 days to reach about 70% of its strength and 28 days for full curing. We recommend light foot traffic after 48 hours and waiting at least 7 days before driving vehicles on a new driveway.

Do you offer decorative concrete options?

+
Yes! We offer a variety of decorative concrete options, including stamped concrete, colored concrete, and exposed aggregate finishes. These techniques can mimic the look of natural stone, brick, or other patterns for highly customized results on patios, walkways, and pool decks.
