Your Outdoor Gathering Hub

Our Fire Pit Services

Custom Fire Pit Design

Every fire pit we build starts with a design conversation. We work with you to choose the size, shape, height, and finish that fits your space and your vision perfectly.

Integrated Seating

Combine your fire pit with built-in concrete seat walls for a complete outdoor gathering space. We design the seating radius for comfortable conversation around the fire.

Gas or Wood-Burning Options

Whether you prefer the ambiance of a wood fire or the convenience of natural gas, we build fire pits for both fuel types with proper venting, clearances, and safety features.

Durable & Heat Resistant

We use refractory materials and proper concrete specifications in fire zones to ensure your fire pit withstands years of high-heat use without cracking, spalling, or deteriorating.

Outdoor Living Integration

Fire pits work best as part of a complete outdoor living plan. We coordinate your fire pit installation with patios, walkways, and landscaping elements for a seamless result.
