Our Garage & Basement Floor Services

New Floor Installation

Complete new concrete floor installation for garages, basements, and utility spaces. We handle subgrade prep, vapor barriers, reinforcement, and finishing to your specifications.

Floor Repair & Resurfacing

Cracked, spalled, or uneven floors can often be restored without full replacement. We resurface damaged concrete to a smooth, attractive, durable finish at a fraction of full replacement cost.

Moisture Barriers

Basement moisture infiltration damages flooring finishes, encourages mold, and creates unhealthy living conditions. We install proper vapor barriers and drainage systems to keep your floor dry.
