Our Patio Services

New Patio Installation

From design consultation through final finishing, we handle every aspect of your new concrete patio. Proper base preparation and drainage are built in from the start.

Patio Repair & Restoration

Cracked, sunken, or spalled patios can often be restored. We assess your existing surface and recommend the most cost-effective path to a beautiful result.

Stamped & Decorative Concrete

Achieve the look of natural stone, brick, or slate at a fraction of the cost. Our stamped concrete work features realistic textures and rich color that hold up to Ohio weather.

Outdoor Living Integration

We coordinate with your landscaping, fire pit, and outdoor kitchen plans to ensure your patio integrates seamlessly into a complete outdoor living environment.

Color & Texture Options

Choose from integral color, acid staining, exposed aggregate, or smooth troweled finishes. We help you select the look that best complements your home and landscaping.
